# What is DERO?
## A Decentralized Application Platform

DERO stands as a versatile decentralized application platform prioritizing privacy and scalability. Developed entirely in Golang by its original creators, DERO's uniqueness lies in its innovation, security, and privacy-focused architecture.

## Privacy-Preserving Features

DERO emphasizes security and privacy, enabling users to conduct confidential transactions and maintain control over their assets without fear of protocol-level or network attacks. Noteworthy features include:

- **Homomorphic Encryption:** Utilizes the first privacy chain to incorporate this technology.
- **Instant Account Balances:** Offers quick access to account balances, minimizing data retrieval from the blockchain.
- **Mining Decentralization:** Eliminates the necessity for mining pools, enhancing network resilience.
- **Erasure Coded Blocks:** Reduces bandwidth requirements and propagates data swiftly, enabling larger block sizes without delays.
- **Efficient Wallets:** Provides lightweight and efficient wallets for user convenience.

## Technical Details

DERO's technical prowess is evident in its cryptographic elements:

- **Bulletproofs:** Implements zero-knowledge range-proofs for enhanced privacy.
- **Cryptonight Hash:** Ensures equal footing for all miners through a memory-bound algorithm.
- **P2P Protocol:** Governs the exchange of blocks, transactions, and the blockchain itself.
- **Pederson Commitment and Borromean Signature:** Integral parts of ring confidential transactions ensuring cryptographic commitment and validation without revealing sensitive data.
- **Homomorphic Encryption and Multilayered Linkable Spontaneous Anonymous Group (MLSAG):** Enhance privacy and validate transaction amounts securely.

## Innovations

#### Client Protocol

**Unique Transaction Handling:** Unlike traditional blockchains, DERO processes transactions individually, allowing the inclusion of blocks containing duplicate or double-spend transactions.

#### DERO Rocket BulletProofs

**Ultrafast Optimizations:** Proprietary optimization techniques enable bulletproof verifications ten times faster than standard implementations, setting new benchmarks in cryptographic performance.

## Mission
DERO is a new blockchain technology written in Golang with a focus on enhanced Privacy and Smart Contracts while maintaining the transparency and security of the blockchain. The goal is to create a unique state of the art blockchain technology with enhanced Reliability, Privacy, Security, Usability, and Portability by bringing together some of the best proven technologies. These technologies will include Homomorphic Encryption, Services and Smart Contracts which will allow for the creation and execution of Decentralized Applications on a Secure Private Blockchain.
