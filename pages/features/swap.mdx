# XMR Atomic Swap 
<Callout emoji="⏲️">
  Be mindful, this process takes about 20 minutes (which is really fast, IMO)

## Before you begin
Please have these things in order before beginning with your swap:
    - Fully synced XMR Wallet
    - Registered DERO Wallet
    - Basic knowledge about DERO CLI Wallet

### Begin the actual swap by pressing (5)
Enter your wallet password then press `Enter`

Paste in the destination address from Step 6 (located at the end) and press `Enter`
It should look like this:
```
dero1qyw4fl3dupcg5qlrcsvcedze507q9u67lxfpu8kgnzp04aq73yheqqg2ctjn4
```
Enter amount to transfer: `1` (for example) and press `Enter`

Enter payment id (or destination port number): `7331` and press `Enter`
<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
Be mindful to paste the correct XMR Deposit Address and nothing else.
</Callout>
Enter Comment:  `PASTE YOUR XMR DEPOSIT ADDRESS HERE` and press `Enter`

### Wait ~20 minutes for XMR to arrive in your XMR Wallet
</Steps>
