# AstroBWT
## A Cryptocurrency Mining Innovation

AstroBWT is an advanced CPU mining algorithm resistant to ASICs, FPGAs, and GPUs, built upon mathematical proofs—a departure from the conventional approaches seen in many other CPU mining algorithms within the cryptocurrency sphere.

### Mathematical Foundation
AstroBWT distinguishes itself through its reliance on rigorous mathematical proofs and research, contrasting with other CPU mining algorithms that hinge on specific computational pathways or conditions prevalent in the field.

### Core Component: Burrows-Wheeler Transform (BWT)
At its core, AstroBWT employs the Burrows-Wheeler Transform (BWT), a cornerstone of Information Theory and the Compression Domains. Despite prolonged research spanning three decades and various attempts to optimize BWT for GPU/FPGA usage, no significant performance enhancements over CPU utilization have been achieved thus far.

### Existing Implementations and Insights
Notably, major technology providers like Intel and NVIDIA have developed optimized versions of BWT, driven by its widespread usage in information theory and compression domains. However, these implementations have failed to demonstrate substantial performance improvements compared to CPU utilization.
