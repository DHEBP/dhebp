## AstroBWT

AstroBWT is an ASIC/FPGA/GPU resistant CPU mining algorithm that is based on mathematical proofs, unlike many other CPU mining algorithms. AstroBWT is an egalitarian algorithm that can run on CPU/GPU/FPGAs and ASICs, however there is little to no economic advantage using specualized hardware.

So far, many ARM CPUs prove to have a better hash to watt ratio than even the highest end GPUs. Mining on mobile devices is not only possible but also fairly common. 

AstroBWT is a mining algorithm that stands out due to its roots in Information Theory and the Compression Domains, setting it apart from many other algorithms used for CPU mining in the cryptocurrency world.

### Why AstroBWT?

**Mathematical Foundation:** Unlike many other CPU mining algorithms that rely on specific computational pathways or conditions, AstroBWT is based on rigorous mathematical proofs and research. This foundation makes it distinct and differentiates it from the standard approaches used in cryptocurrency mining.

**Core Component - Burrows-Wheeler Transform (BWT):** AstroBWT is built upon the Burrows-Wheeler Transform, commonly known as BWT. BWT has been an area of active research for about three decades, especially in the fields of Information Theory and Compression Domains. Despite numerous attempts to optimize BWT for GPU/FPGA usage, no significant improvements over CPU performance have been achieved thus far.

**Existing Implementations and Studies:** Major technology providers such as Intel and NVIDIA have already developed optimized versions of BWT due to its frequent usage in information theory and compression. However, the implementations available to date haven't shown substantial performance enhancements compared to CPU performance.

**Potential Impact Beyond Mining:** The optimization or significant performance boost of AstroBWT on FPGAs, ASICs, or GPUs could have broader implications beyond cryptocurrency mining. Improvements in AstroBWT could lead to advancements in domains like Bioinformatics, Signal Processing, DNA Sequencing, and more. This is because BWT is used extensively in these scientific research fields.

**Resistant Algorithm with Broader Benefits:** AstroBWT aims not only to resist ASICs, FPGAs, and GPUs in cryptocurrency mining but also to contribute to scientific research if optimized for these platforms. Its potential improvement could pave the way for advancements in various scientific domains where BWT finds applications.

AstroBWT innovates by blending theoretical concepts from Information Theory and Compression with practical mining. It leverages the Burrows-Wheeler Transform (BWT) for mining, aiming to surpass CPU performance. 

Its impact extends beyond mining, potentially advancing scientific domains like Bioinformatics and Signal Processing. The focus is on optimizing BWT for broader computational tasks, distinguishing it from previous attempts and algorithms.
