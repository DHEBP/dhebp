# What are dApps?
dApps, or Decentralized Applications, are applications usually in the form of financial services, art projects, video games, or other various applications that are built using decentralized blockchain technology. Due to the decentralized nature of these applications, it makes it much more difficult for a governing authority to impose restrictions, censor ideas, or in any way alter or obstruct the use of the application.

In the Dero ecosystem, dApps can easily be developed using the custom [DVM-Basic](/features/dvm) Smart Contract language.
