## How to Create a Paper Wallet

A paper wallet is a secure offline storage method. It involves generating a wallet's public address, private spend key, view key, and mnemonic seed on an air-gapped computer. The information is then printed on paper or stored in a secure format such as a PDF or text file. They are typically used for long-term storage and require caution to prevent loss or unauthorized access.

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  Ensure that you download the wallet software from the official Dero website (dero.io) or from trusted sources.
</Callout>

<Steps>
 
### Download the Paper Wallet Generator
 
Visit the official Dero paper wallet generator at [dero.io](https://dero.io/). This is a trusted and widely used tool for creating Dero paper wallets.

### Disconnect from the Internet
 
For enhanced security, it's recommended to perform the following steps on an offline or air-gapped computer. Disconnect your computer from the internet to minimize the risk of any potential online threats.

### Generate your Wallet
 
Follow the instructions on the website to generate a new Monero paper wallet. You'll typically be asked to move your mouse or randomly input characters to generate the wallet's cryptographic keys.

### Print or Write Down you Wallet Details
 
Once the paper wallet is generated, you'll be provided with the public address (to receive funds) and the corresponding private spend key (to access and spend the funds). Print out the generated paper wallet or carefully write down the wallet details on a physical piece of paper. Make sure to keep this paper in a secure and private location.

### Test your New Wallet
 
To ensure the accuracy of the paper wallet, you can send a small amount of DERO to the generated public address and then use a blockchain explorer (e.g., [Block Explorer](https://explorer.dero.io/)) to verify the transaction and balance associated with the address.

### Store your Paper Wallet Securely
 
Treat your paper wallet as you would treat cash or valuable documents. Store it in a secure and protected location, preferably in a sealed envelope or a tamper-evident bag to detect any unauthorized access.

</Steps>
<Callout type="info" emoji="ℹ️">
  Remember to take necessary precautions to safeguard your paper wallet and never share your private spend key with anyone.
</Callout>
